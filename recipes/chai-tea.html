Milk Chai

This milk chai recipe is a comforting and aromatic Indian tea made with tata tea, milk, and sugar.
Perfect for chilly mornings or relaxing evenings, this classic beverage is easy to make at home.
Enjoy a cup of homemade chai with your favorite snacks or breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 cups water
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 table spoon of tea from tata tea pack
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, add water, Tea powder Bring to a boil.
  2. Pour in the milk and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.
  3. Add sugar and stir until dissolved. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  4. Strain the chai into cups and serve hot.
